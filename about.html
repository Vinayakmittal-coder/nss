About NSS NSUT

Committed to Service, Leadership, and Social Responsibility

Our Mission

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at NSUT aims to foster the spirit of volunteerism and social commitment in students. We promote holistic development through hands-on service and civic engagement.

Our Vision

To empower the youth of NSUT to become active participants in the development of the nation and uplift communities through consistent outreach and impactful initiatives.

Objectives

  • Instill a sense of social responsibility and leadership in students.
  • Develop personality through community service.
  • Promote awareness about social, environmental, and health issues.
  • Strengthen the bond between academia and society.
  • Foster discipline, cooperation, and national pride.
